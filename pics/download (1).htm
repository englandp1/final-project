Description

A luxurious creamy base to create the perfect blank canvas ready for ultimate colour popping creations. With its super thick consistency and no cracking quality, the P.Louise Base is a must have product to have in your kit. Its full coverage formula helps to cancel out any unwanted brow hair and carve out those highly requested fleeky brows.Selecting a suitable base shade using online swatches can be tricky, so we have developed a ‘Shade Guide’ using a well recognised worldwide cosmetic brand to help. Base Shade 4 is suitable for somebody who wears a MAC 43.5 foundation.
